## Identity Locks

The following features must remain consistent in every frame and animation state:

1. Two large upward-curving yellow horns with bold black tiger-like stripes.
2. Two smaller pale ivory horns on the forehead.
3. Orange-red eyes with thick black angled eyebrows.
4. Bright orange body with saturated yellow fire highlights.
5. Thick black collar with evenly spaced silver spikes.
6. Two small black bat wings with orange edge accents.
7. Oversized flame-shaped hands with yellow claws.
8. Black smoky lower body instead of legs.
9. Lower body tapers into a curved tail with a yellow-orange pointed tip.
10. Compact muscular body, large head, and clearly readable mascot silhouette.

## Color Rules

Use a vivid, highly saturated fire palette:

- Main body: bright saturated orange
- Fire highlights: glowing yellow and golden yellow
- Deep fire shadows: red-orange
- Wings, collar, horn stripes and lower body: charcoal black
- Small horns and collar spikes: pale ivory / silver
- Eyes: orange-red with light yellow highlights

Do not mute, pastelize, or significantly darken the palette.

## Animation Direction

### idle

Calm hovering pose. Subtle breathing, one natural blink, slight fire flicker in the hands, and a small tail sway. Keep the motion restrained and loopable.

### running-right

Move energetically toward the right. The smoky lower body and tail should trail behind. Wings and flame hands may bounce slightly. The face should remain determined.

### running-left

A correct left-facing counterpart of running-right. Preserve horn stripes, collar spikes, markings, lighting logic, and character identity.

### waving

Raise one flame hand in an impatient but friendly greeting. The gesture should be readable without detached fire effects.

### jumping

A brief upward celebration or excited power surge. Compress before rising and return cleanly to the starting pose.

### failed

Frustrated and embarrassed rather than injured. Shoulders and wings droop, flame hands dim slightly, eyes look disappointed or annoyed.

### waiting

Tilt the head slightly and hover impatiently. Small tail movement and restrained finger or flame movement. It should communicate that user input is required.

### running

Represents Codex actively working, not physical directional running. Show focused rapid work through controlled hand movement, brighter internal fire, alert eyes, and an energetic hover.

### review

Represents carefully reviewing completed work. Narrow the eyes slightly, lean forward, and use a restrained inspecting gesture. Do not add readable documents, text, screens, or UI.

## Sprite Constraints

- One character only.
- Full body must remain inside every frame.
- Transparent background in final animation assets.
- No floor shadows.
- No floating sparks separated from the body.
- No speed lines, smoke trails, impact marks, glow halos, or detached effects.
- No readable text, symbols, speech bubbles, UI, tools, or additional characters.
- Keep scale and baseline stable between adjacent frames.
- Keep all important features readable at small Codex pet size.
